The digital videogame distribution management system (DVDMS) is to simplify the day-to-day activities of a software distributer like in providing newest games to the users, keeping a record of the games owned by the users, updating the records, and sometimes helping in changing certain details.

Also, this system keeps a check on all the videogame publishers whether distribution of their games is profitable for the distributer or not. Since such activities takes place daily, the system provides efficient and fast way to accomplish these tasks. 

This system helps in reducing paperwork and saves time. It helps in automating the whole videogame distribution system.
